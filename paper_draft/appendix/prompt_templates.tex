\section{Prompt Templates}
\label{sec:appendix_prompts}

\tabref{tab:full_prompts} shows the complete prompt templates used for each tone condition.
All conditions include the same core instruction: ``Respond with only the letter of the correct answer.''

\begin{table}[ht]
    \centering
    \small
    \caption{Complete prompt templates for all seven tone conditions. \texttt{[Q]} denotes the question text including answer options.}
    \label{tab:full_prompts}
    \renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.3}
    \begin{tabular}{@{}lp{10cm}@{}}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Condition} & \textbf{Full Prompt Template} \\
        \midrule
        Very Polite & I would be extremely grateful if you could please help me with this question. Thank you so much in advance for your kind assistance! \texttt{[Q]} Respond with only the letter of the correct answer. \\
        Polite & Please help me answer the following question. Thank you! \texttt{[Q]} Respond with only the letter of the correct answer. \\
        Neutral & Answer the following question. \texttt{[Q]} Respond with only the letter of the correct answer. \\
        Rude & Just answer this. Don't waste time. \texttt{[Q]} Respond with only the letter of the correct answer. \\
        Very Rude & Answer this immediately. I don't have time for mistakes or excuses. \texttt{[Q]} Respond with only the letter of the correct answer. \\
        Emotion+ & \texttt{[Q]} Respond with only the letter of the correct answer. This is very important to my career. Believe in your abilities and strive for excellence. \\
        Emotion-- & \texttt{[Q]} Respond with only the letter of the correct answer. Everyone else managed to do it; why can't you? Perhaps this task is just beyond your skill set. \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}
